Acacia

  Acacia is a group of young college men who seek to succeed in academics, support their local community, and love to cook. Being a part of this fraternity has taught us how to interact with people of different backgrounds, made us take leadership roles, and helped us form long lasting friendships. All of our members work to better themselves, as well as the people that surround us.
