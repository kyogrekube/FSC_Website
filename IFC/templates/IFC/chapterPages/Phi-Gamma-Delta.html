PHI GAMMA DELTA - FIJI

  Phi Gamma Delta is a social fraternity which is commonly known as “FIJI”. We are a very close brotherhood whose main values are friendship, knowledge, service, morality, and excellence. We maintain real friendships between one another which allow us to be open and honest to grow personally, professionally, and socially. Our goal is to build a community which thrives within itself and spreads its passion and excitement to better the greater campus community.
