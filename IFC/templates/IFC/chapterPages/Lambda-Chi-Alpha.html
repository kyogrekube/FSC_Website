Lambda Chi Alpha

  Our fraternity's mission is to inspire and equip men to leave ethical lives of growth, service, and leadership. Our chapter’s vision is to build up the membership, ideals, and spirit of this chapter so that affiliation shall ever be regarded as an honor and a privilege.
