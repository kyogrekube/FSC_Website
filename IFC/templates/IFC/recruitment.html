Why Join A Greek Organization?

Joining a greek organization provides a unique opportunity to cultivate lifelong friendships, foster personal growth, and create a strong network of professional connections. As a member of our greek community, you will have access to leadership development programs, community service projects, and social events that promote camaraderie and teamwork. Our fraternity and sorority commons is committed to creating a respectful and inclusive environment that encourages individuality while also emphasizing shared values and goals. By becoming part of our greek system, you are not only joining a brotherhood but investing in a future filled with support, success, and enrichment.

Community

Joining a greek organization is more than a college experience; it's a lifelong journey filled with camaraderie, personal growth, and community involvement. Engage in enriching social events, make connections that last a lifetime, and contribute to meaningful philanthropic efforts. By becoming part of this vibrant community, you'll forge bonds that extend beyond your university years. Discover the unique opportunities and friendships that await you in greek life!

Our Chapters

Philanthropy & Service

Philanthropy and service are at the heart of our commitment to both the RPI community and the broader Troy community. Through hands-on volunteering, organized charity events, and collaboration with local organizations, we strive to make a positive impact. Join us in making a difference!
