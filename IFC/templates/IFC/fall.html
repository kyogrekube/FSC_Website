For potential new members (PNMs), please adhere to the following rules:

  1. In the fall semester, only second-year students or those in higher years are eligible to join a fraternity. It is mandatory for all students intending to participate in recruitment or join a fraternity to register with the IFC
  2. Eligibility for recruitment requires a cumulative GPA of 2.5 or higher if you are a returning RPI student.
  3. You must not be an initiated brother or full member of any other national fraternity, whether at RPI or from another campus.
  4. If you are a transfer student and wish to affiliate with the chapter you joined at your previous institution, please contact the RPI Chapter President or email fscommons@rpi.edu to get connected.
